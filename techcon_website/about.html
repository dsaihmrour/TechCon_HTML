Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small meetup of software engineers into one of the world’s most anticipated annual tech events. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees and become a launchpad for breakthrough technologies and collaborations.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to empower tech professionals and enthusiasts to explore, innovate, and lead. We believe in creating a space where ideas turn into impact, and communities flourish through shared knowledge and opportunity.

Past Speakers

Each year, TechCon welcomes visionary leaders who have made significant contributions to the technology industry:

Dr. Anita Roberts

A pioneer in AI ethics and data privacy, Dr. Roberts has shaped international policies around responsible AI development.

James Wu

As the founder of GreenCode, James is known for creating sustainable software practices that reduce digital carbon footprints.

Sara Al-Khaled

Sara has led global open-source initiatives and is recognized for her work in making technology more accessible in developing countries.
